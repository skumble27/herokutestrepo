Health of a Nation

Dashboard Overview

The health of a nation can be assessed by a number of parameters that extend beyond the basic medical definitions of health. Although major factors such as mental health, life expectancy, health spans as well as obesity and other such issues, other aspects should be taken into equal consideration. As such, this dashboard provides a snapshot view of nations performances in three key critical areas, namely, health, agriculture and environmental impact as these factors do not work in a mutually exclusive manner and can possibly contribute to the overall health of a nation. In order to obtain key information of various countries, click on the nation provided in the map below to retrieve the data.
